About Kisanmart


Purplecrow Company is formed to serve the customers by adding value on every purchase and service, the company has vision to make the products available to customers direct from fields and Producers. To enable this the company has setup “Kisanmart” a retail chain, for customers to visit and feel the product and make it available through e-commerce model as WWW.KISANMART000WEBHOST.VOM , with an online model that curtails costs saved in Rents/Illumination and other operational costs which will be passed on to the customer. Also the company has Direct Delivery Model as “Growersmart” to link the producer directly to the customer. The vision is to eliminate various types of costs involved like middle men margins, real estate costs, lighting and operational costs and sell the products at affordable costs with superior quality and add value to the customers.
